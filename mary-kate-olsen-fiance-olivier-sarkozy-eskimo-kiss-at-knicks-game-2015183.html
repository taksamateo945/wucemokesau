Mary-Kate Olsen, Fiance Olivier Sarkozy Eskimo Kiss at Knicks Game

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Enough to melt igloos! Mary-Kate Olsen and her fiancé Olivier Sarkozy packed on the PDA while watching their beloved New York Knicks take on the San Antonio Spurs at Madison Square Garden in NYC on Tuesday, Mar. 17.

The Full House alum, 28, and her significantly older fiance, 45, nuzzled noses during the game and stared longingly into each other’s eyes. Olsen dressed casually for the sports event, wearing a black leather moto jacket and pulling her blonde tresses into a messy top knot.

Her financier fiance, clad in a fur-lined jacket, sporadically made ridiculous faces with his forehead pressed up against Olsen’s temple.

Aside from canoodling throughout the game, the sports enthusiasts rooted on the Knicks. At one point, Olsen touched her face, giving a very clear and direct view of her massive vintage Cartier engagement ring. The sparkler, which Olsen placed on her right ring finger for the outing, was reportedly purchased for $81,250 at a Sotheby’s auction.
