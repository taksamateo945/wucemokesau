Kourtney Kardashian Introduces Son Reign Aston Disick: First Photo!

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Reign-ing in our hearts! Though Kourtney Kardashian gave birth to her third child on Dec. 14, 2014, the reality star, 35, didn’t share a picture of the tiny tot until Thursday, April 2. 

“My little turtle dove angel baby boy Reign Aston Disick,” she captioned the Instagram shot of her second-born son in a light grey bundl onesie.

Reign was born on the fifth birthday of Kardashian and Scott Disick’s first son Mason. The couple also shares daughter Penelope, 2.
