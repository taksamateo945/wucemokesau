Nicki Minaj Hosted the 2014 MTV EMAs in 10 Outfits: Photos

Publish date: 2024-02-26

Wardrobe, please!  

Nicki Minaj is a lot more high-fashion these days, so of course the rapper would require no less than 10 outfits to host the 2014 MTV EMAs in Glasgow, Scotland on Sunday, Nov. 9.  

In case you missed it — see all of her very different looks, from the longest skirt to ever open an award show to her most risqué bodysuit yet (which is saying a lot!).  

Float On in That Mullet Skirt 

The host, 31, opened the show suspended in the air in a bedazzled silver bra with a long, ruffled mullet skirt by Peter Hidalgo. The “Anaconda” singer’s ample cleavage nearly upstaged that skirt.  

Ravishing in Red 

Minaj kept the party going in a hot red mini with a shell design that flared out in the cutest way.  

Midnight Sparkle 

Young Money’s fearless first lady had a regal moment on stage in a black floor-length gown with a sheer bodice.  

Winter White Done Right 

Rocking one of her favorite designers, the striking Trinidad-born star got flirty in a sheer Roberto Cavalli minidress with side cutouts.  

Sexiest Jumpsuit Ever 

Rules? When it comes to Minaj’s outrageous fashion, there are no rules. The pint-size rapper kept it sexy in a sheer black jumpsuit, revealing her dainty undergarments. 

Still a Harajuku Barbie 

Minaj donned a short black wig and sported a black and white cape ensemble to greet the audience. “GARETH PUGH lookin like I’m in the matrix with some bad btchs,” she wrote on Instagram.  

Hole in One 

The “Save Me” songstress got cutesy in a prep-tastic Alexander Wang green and white dress. The short black wig made another appearance. 

Gorgeous in Green 

The rapper interacted with the crowd in a sparkly green halter dress. 

Glitz Galore 

Minaj wowed the crowd in head-to-toe sparkles courtesy of Saint Laurent.
