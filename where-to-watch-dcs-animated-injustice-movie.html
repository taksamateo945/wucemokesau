Where to Watch DCs Animated Injustice Movie

Publish date: 2024-06-17

Your favorite DC heroes and villains are back for a new animated adventure in the upcoming film Injustice.

Based on the video game Injustice: Gods Among Us and the graphic novel Injustice: Gods Among Us: Year One, the film takes place in a world where the Joker (Kevin Pollak) has tricked Superman (Justin Hartley) into killing Lois Lane (Laura Bailey). As a result, the world has been plunged into chaos, and Superman embarks on a dangerous quest to control it as a result. It’s up to Batman (Anson Mount) to assemble a team of heroes to stop the caped hero and save the world.

Curious when Injustice will be released, and how to watch it at home? Read on to find out.

What is the Injustice release date?

Injustice will be released on Tuesday, Oct. 19, 2021.

Will Injustice be on a streaming service?

No, sorry. Injustice is a straight to digital and Blu-Ray release. It’s available as part of a 4K Ultra HD Blu-ray Combo Package, which retails for $39.99. In comparison, a Blu-ray copy costs $22.99, while a digital Prime Video copy costs $19.99.

You can preorder the film on Amazon now.

When will Injustice be available to stream for free?

Right now, it’s unclear when Injustice will be available on a streaming service. However, HBO Max is currently the exclusive U.S. streaming home of DC content, so there’s a good chance that the film will arrive there eventually.
